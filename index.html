How Native Dukan Works

The easiest way to get exactly what you are looking for.

1. Post a Request

Upload a photo of your list or type in the products you need. Select your category and hit submit.

2. Get Local Offers

Nearby partner stores review your request and send back offers with pricing and availability.

3. Choose & Receive

Accept the best offer. Choose to pick it up directly from the store or have it delivered to your door.
